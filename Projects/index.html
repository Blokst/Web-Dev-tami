What are cats?
According to Oxford Dictionary: "A well-known carnivorous quadruped (Felis domesticus) which has long been domesticated, being kept to destroy mice, and as a house pet.""

Facts on Cats

  • More is known about the cat`s sensory system than any other animal
  • Research using cats has helped develop cochlear implants
  • Cats have retractable claws that they use for climbing and hunting.
  • A group of cats is called a "clowder".
  • Cats have "carpal whiskers" on the back of their front legs that help them detect objects and navigate areas.

Cat Breeds

There are many different types of cat breeds, you might be wondering what they are.
According to Brittanica: Cat breeds are a group of related felines that share a conformation, meaning a similar physical appearance, or a common geographic region of origin.
